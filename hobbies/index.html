Chess

Chess has become my latest obsession. Itâ€™s easy to play and impossible to master. Just the way I like my games!

As a beginner to studying chess myself, I highly recommend the following:

  • Chess.com Guides for "New to Chess" and "Beginner"
  • Chessable.com free courses like Chess Basics
  • Smity's Opening Fundamentals (the Intro and Section I are incredibly useful)
  • DO learn about opening fundamentals. DO NOT spend time this early on with memorizing opening moves and variations.
  • Be patient! At the very least think if your move will give up a piece for free (blunder checks).
  • Learn chess notation, notate your games, and review them against a chess engine afterwards. You'll find common areas where you are making mistakes and can focus on those spots more.
